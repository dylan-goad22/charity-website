Our Mission

The Teach A Man To Fish Foundation is a non-profit organization that commits time and funds to creating better lives for the less fortunate in our society. Despite our name, we help everyone without discrimination. Hardships target everyone, and we want to help everyone overcome them.

Our teams travel across America and find those who are homeless, get them cleaned up and well fed, and then start a process. Our headquarters in Dallas, Texas serves as a staging grounds for helping those individuals. If they agree to our proposal, the selected individuals will be housed and trained in several different fields of their choosing. Once they complete the program, we help them settle in the career world to start their renewed life!

Who We've Helped

Jeffrey

This is Jeffrey. He was found in Dallas, Texas begging the local businessmen for spare change. When we approached him with the opportunity to become successful, he was ecstatic. He followed through with the program and now Jeffrey no longer begs those businessmen because he became one himself.

Martin

Martin was nominated by his concerned family. Our team picked him up in Fort Worth, Texas and immediately gave him help. He was transported back to Dallas temporarily and he began training to have his dream job, a business owner. He opened up a barber shop and now assists TAMTF Foundation with cleaning up more individuals that are brought into our program.
